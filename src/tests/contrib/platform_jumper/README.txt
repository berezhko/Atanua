Open PlatformJumper.atanua to play.
Use Zoom ext to find the circuit and zoom in on the LED array.

You start in the top left corner.
Try to survive by not falling off the bottom of the screen.
Use a/d to move left and right and w to jump.
Press r to start/restart.

Made for the Ludum Dare 48h game design contest #26
by Daniel Morrison <daniel@mptr.com>
